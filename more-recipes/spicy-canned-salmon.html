Spicy Canned Salmon

Description: Canned salmon mixed with rice vinegar, Sriracha, and mayonnaise makes this one spicy salmon salad rice bowl. Fresh cucumbers, quickly marinated in soy sauce, and sliced avocado add some coolness. Feel free to make this bowl your own by adding more ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped English cucumber.
  • 1 tablespoon low-sodium soy sauce.
  • 1 (14¾ ounce) can of pink salmon, skin and bone removed, drained and flaked.
  • 2½ tablespoons of chile-garlic sauce (such as Sriracha©).
  • 1 tablespoon of light mayonnaise.
  • 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar.
  • 1 cup cooked white rice.
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and sliced.
  • ½ teaspoon of sesame seeds.
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped green onion for garnish (optional).

Directions

  1. Combined chopped cucumbers and soy sauce in a small and set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the salmon, Sriracha©, mayonnaise, and rice vinegar.
  3. In a bowl place cooked rice and top it with the salmon mixture, sliced avocado, and chopped cucumber mixture. Sprinkle with sesame seeds. Top with extra Sriracha and green onions if desired.
